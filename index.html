What we do

At Secure North Ltd, we specialize in providing premier security solutions tailored to each client's unique needs. Our team combines years of expertise with state-of-the-art technology to deliver comprehensive services, including manned guarding, surveillance, and custom security strategies. We are committed to ensuring robust protection for your assets and peace of mind. Based in the community's heart, we build lasting relationships with our clients, offering reliable and efficient security services you can trust.

Our Experience

With over 10 years of experience in the security industry, we have become experts in providing top-notch security guard services to our clients.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of security guard services to meet the unique needs of each of our clients, including event security, mobile patrols, and more.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer robust, customized security solutions that guarantee our clients’ safety and peace of mind. Utilizing advanced technology and deep industry knowledge, we provide dependable and thorough security services, creating a secure environment for both businesses and communities.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly trained and professional security guards who are dedicated to keeping our clients safe and secure.

Our Values

Our core values are the foundation of our commitment to safety and security. We uphold the highest ethical standards with unwavering integrity in all our operations, ensuring transparency and trust. Our team is constantly vigilant, keeping a keen eye on safety to anticipate and mitigate risks effectively. We embrace innovation, continually advancing our technology and methods to address modern security challenges, while our deep commitment to the community drives us to protect and enhance the environments where we work and live.

Our Integrity

Accredited by the SIA as an Approved Contractor, all our security personnel are fully licensed and receive training that adheres to the rigorous standards audited by The British Assessment Bureau. Our commitment extends beyond basic guarding services, as we actively support our staff in building rewarding careers within the security industry. We continuously invest in advanced training methods to stay ahead of the dynamic demands of the private security sector. These efforts, built on a solid base of experience, reliability, and consistency, provide us with a clear advantage in the industry.
